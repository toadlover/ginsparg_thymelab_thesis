State selection:
Selected states 6 and 12 from PDB 1SXE due to having well exposed TYR194 and GLU122
Used pymol to extract those states to individual PDBs

Binding pocket shape creation:
Using Rosetta LigandDiscoverySearch to spit out space fill matrices around the binding pocket area to create shapes of the binding pocket
Example arg file args_state_6_binding_shape:
